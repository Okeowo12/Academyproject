PRITCHARD SCHOOL CAMPUS

CAMPUS LOCATION & CONTACT INFORMATION

220 Pritchard Avenue, Winnipeg
204-589-6885
elementary.office@faithacademy.ca

Our Pritchard Avenue campus is a kindergarten to grade 4 program. It is a community school for those living in the immediate area.
The building features three classrooms as well as an indoor gym and outdoor play area. The education program includes chapels, physical education, swimming program and Bible class. Grades 3 and 4 also participate in French class. When possible the students participate together in chapels and fun events with the Matheson school.

Transportation is provided for all students attending the Pritchard location.
