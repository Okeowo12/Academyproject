CAMPUS LOCATION & CONTACT INFORMATION

SCHOOL CALENDER

437 Matheson Avenue

204-582-3400

204-582-2616

elementary.office@faithacademy.ca

Kindergarten to Grade 4 are located at the east side of the Matheson Campus. They are separated from the high school program by a school gym.



The elementary program includes chapels, physical education, swimming program and Bible class. Grade 1 to 4 also are included in the French program. Students are respected and taught to respect others and every subject is taught consistent with recognizing the authority of God’s Word.
