PARENT ACCESS TO EDSEMBLI

Grades 9 to 12 are located on the west side of the Matheson Campus. They are separated from the K-4 Elementary program by a school gym.



We offer a quality education program focused on the spiritual and moral development of every child. The curriculum includes chapels, Christian service and ongoing missions training. Sports programs are emphasized with a concentration on volleyball, basketball and soccer teams. The high school program also includes House Groups which provide each student with a chance to connect with students from other grades. House Groups are led by staff members and are used in intramurals, chapel discussion and games, as well as other school events. They have been designed to promote unity and school spirit.


Teachers are committed to fostering an ongoing relationship with each student. Students are expected to follow Standards of Dress and Conduct which are detailed in our School Guide.


Students in Grades 9 to 12 take courses to earn credits toward graduation. In order to graduate with a diploma from the Province of Manitoba, students must earn a minimum of 30 credits from a combination of compulsory and elective courses. To earn a Faith Academy Diploma, students must earn a minimum of 32 credits which must include the Christian Service credit and the Doctrine credits from the years a student attended Faith Academy.


Additional information about the high school is available in the High School Course Catalogue and the High School Student Handbook available from the office.
