EMPLOYMENT

Faith Academy is a small community school which provides a compassionate and caring environment for its students. We seek to impart the values that will serve as a foundation for a biblical worldview, while helping students grow to be devoted followers of Christ who, “do justly, love mercy, walk humbly with your God” Micah 6:8.

Faith Academy offers a culture of positivity and a supportive atmosphere for employees. Many of the staff have worked at the school for years and new members are easily welcomed. We welcome applications from people who share our values and love for education.

Requirements for Employment:

Candidates for employment at Faith Academy must be committed to their Christian faith and have a strong work ethic. If you wish to be considered for a position, please submit a resume, personal testimony, and philosophy of Christian education to the Superintendent at superintendent@faithacademy.ca.

CURRENT POSITIONS

Only those candidates that are selected for an interview will be contacted. Please refer to the requirements above when submitting your application. Only candidates who have fully submitted all the requirements will be considered for an open position.

SUBSTITUTE TEACHERS

Faith Academy is accepting applications for the role of substitute teacher at all levels.
Please indicate which grade levels you are interested in when applying.
Successful candidates will possess a Manitoba Teaching Certificate.
A child abuse registry and criminal record check will also be required.

BUS DRIVER

The Successful candidate will either have or be willing to work towards a School Bus Driver Certificate and a valid class 2 License.
The position includes 4 hours of driving a day - 2hrs in the day and 2 hrs after school.
A child abuse registry and criminal record check will also be required.
Please submit your resume to chris.franczyk@faithacademy.ca.
Applications will be accepted until the position is filled.
